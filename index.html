About

We’re a community of labels, collectives, bloggers and lots more… in short, open-minded, creative people with a passion for music.

We want to help promote music that we genuinely believe in, and that’s why we decided to create a membership-only platform to give exposure to talented artists. But it’s not just about that – it’s also about building a community of like-minded people and opening the door for collaborations, opportunities and friendships.

To achieve that, we have set a broadcasting space, where we stream live through our website the sounds that identify our members, uploading all content to our youtube and soundcloud channels; we have as set a record label , releasing digital and physical material, where we showcase and release talented producers from all over the world with the aim to expose and bring a new groove into the scene mixing a range of genres and rhythms. And last – but by no means least collaborating every month with all members in our events at the Total Refreshment Centre with our events and BBQ’s in summer at the outdoors space.

There are several reasons why we got started – but all of them link back to one: our passion for good music. There’s a lot of good content being produced, and as much as the internet has proved to be a great tool for making it accessible to everyone, it’s also made it harder for the good stuff to get the attention it deserves and reach the right ears. We think music isn’t just a product and consuming it shouldn’t be a competition – rather, we want to encourage people to look deeper, to take an active interest in music. Part of that needs action from the industry itself, and that’s why we want to make it more accesible for musicians, labels, collectives and anyone else involved in the scene to connect and collaborate with each other and to spread the word about the things we believe in.

Cadrave

last Wednesday 8pm - 9pm

Definition of a “Cadavre Exquis”: Also known as Exquisite corpse or rotating corpse, a “Cadavre Exquis” is an artistic method by which a collection of words or images (and now sounds) is collectively assembled. Each collaborator adds to a composition in sequence, either by following a rule or theme or by being allowed to see only the end of what the previous person contributed.

Curated by graphic artist Samad Jble, CDXQ aims to arouse your curiosity with randomised musical themes. A word, a date, a place… even a dish, can be the subject for an hour of musical representation.

Flumo Recordings

Last Thursday 6pm - 8pm

Established in southern European city of Seville, Spain in 2006, Flumo has since shown never stopping growth of creativity that has made it an important and valuable institution within the underground dance scene. Currently locat- ed in London the label continues to offer quality music for those who seek something more ardent and exquisite. Those who are attracted to such sounds come from various walks of life, from media professionals, record col- lectors, DJs and of course the dancers. The deep seated community of artists that has built the label as it is today are from many walks of lives themselves and therefore bring a different take on the current techno and house scenes in which they are involved such as ones located in Japan, South Africa, the Americas and various corners of Europe.

2015 is scheduled to be a great year for the label, bringing newcomers and established artists together for more exclusive music that will be released digi- tally and physically. Flumo will continue to promote and encourage innovation in new artists while also filling the shelves of your local record stores with grooves from a wide variety of internationally renown names such as Gari Romalis, Borrowed Identity, Esteban Adame, El_Txef_A, Dixie Yure and many more.

Inside Out Records

2nd Monday 5pm - 7pm

Alessandro Lombardo aka Alex Zed has been a part of electronic music and club culture for over 15 years. Alex benefited from his parents vast eclectic record collection and so started from a very young age. Uniquely accomplished in both influence and scope, he has excelled in every one of his incubations: DJ, label owner and producer.

At the age of 18 he started playing at local parties of his hometown. Originally hailing from Sicily, Alex soon found the club scene of London irresistible, and so relocated at the age 25 to try his hand behind the decks of Mass club in Brixton. He has shared the decks with such techno luminaries as Mauro Picotto, Dave the Drummer and Chris Liberator. Soon he started organizing his own weekly events in underground venues and warehouses in East London.

International music entrepreneurs have noticed his mixing skills and Alex was invited to play at the finest clubs including The City, Cancun – Transit, Philadelphia – The Vagabond, Miami as well as numerous venues across Europe. He has since enjoyed immense success, blowing up dancefloors across the globe (especially in UK) to sold-out-crowds. Alex is also a prolific and respected producer,having released several EPs in "Authenticbit" such as 8Joyz ,Sweet Vibration and others.

Recently, his dream of creating his own music label have come true. Inside Out Records is a new label established in London,UK that meets great success from the beginning. He is collaborating with important names of the field in remixes and EPs that will soon be released.

Alex's musical style reflects his eclectic influences, all distilled down to pure,crystalline deep house. He's not limited to deep house, instead combining tech house and techno to create a new unique sound.

Kasia

1st Monday 5pm -7pm

Born and raised in Poland, living in London, Kasia discovered electronic music in in the early nineties. After moving to London in 2001 she quickly became enamored with the club scene; inspired by the music she was hearing, and underground parties she was going to, she got her turntables and started collecting and playing her first vinyl records.

Her first professional step into the electronic music world was in London in 2009. After her first DJ gig in she started working her way through the underground party circuit in East London playing gigs, at small intimate venues and underground warehouse parties

Her DJ sets reflect her eclectic taste, delving into a world of weird and challenging music. Her sound could be heard as somewhere between from house, dub to techno with a healthy dose of deep groove and funk thrown into the mix.

Over last few years her DJ travels have taken her to countries such as Germany, Holland, Portugal, and Croatia.

Kasia is currently producing and writing music as part of duo called KP Sessions, and since then had released music on Release/Sustain.

Little Beat Different

2nd & last Wednesday 9pm - 11pm

Little Beat Different is a biweekly radio show curated by LBD crew - Kozber, Oli_N and Steamfunk. Show is running every 2nd & 4th Wednesday of the month 21:00 - 23:00 (GMT).

Living, producing, playing, promoting and breathing music for many years, LBD is aiming to bring some fresh air to London’s crowded digital airwaves. The programme aims to showcase the eclectic tastes of its creators, ranging from laidback electronica, through soul, jazz and funk tunes to straight-up deep house and techno.

The show will not be limited to new releases in any way, although the introduction of new and up-and-coming releases will be regular feature. Instead, the DJs will often look back in time, reflecting their crate-digging past and present. Also, there will be dedicated space for some exciting guest DJs and producers, exclusive interviews and unreleased tracks previews. All good demo submissions are to be considered for airplay as well, the high quality being the only criteria. Behind the curtain, LBD crew is quietly cooking its own label plans too, which will no doubt make demo selection even more attractive.

Shumba Maasai

Shumba Maasai delivers a blend of conscious and introspective poetry, drenched in contemporary attitude. Heady and menacing beats back-up vocals that flicker from Shumba's tranquillised inner thoughts to militant metaphors fired at the listener. With a high lyrical ability, unorthodox approach to writing and a strong poetic element throughout, the lyrics are just as comfortable in spoken word as they are when lacing the beat.

We Play The Music We Love

3rd Wednesday 6pm - 8pm

We Play The Music We Love has been built to provide a place where artists are encouraged to push the boundaries of their craft and to promote shared values of respect, acceptance, and peacefulness through the stories they tell. It is dedicated to the idea that the differences among us should be celebrated, but that our shared humanity, at the end, unites us as one. Our goal is to make the world a little more interesting and a little gentler.
